1. Melt butter over medium heat in a non-stick frying pan.
2. Crack two eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork until they are mixed well.
3. Pour the eggs into the frying pan and use a spatula to spread it out evenly.
4. Cook the omelette for around 2-3 minutes or until the top of the omelette is almost solid.
5. Add the ham slices and creamy cheese to one side of the omelette.
6. Use a spatula to fold the other side of the omelette over the toppings.
7. Let the omelette cook for another 1-2 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
8. Drizzle the thick sauce honey over the top of the omelette before serving.
Your omelette with sliced ham, creamy cheese, and thick sauce honey is ready to serve!
