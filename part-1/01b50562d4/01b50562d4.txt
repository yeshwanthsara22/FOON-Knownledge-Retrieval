1. Start by gathering your ingredients: whole leafy spinach, sliced strawberry, halved orange, sliced olive, chopped nut, thick sauce honey, thick sauce mustard, and liquid oil.
2. Rinse and dry the spinach leaves and place them in a large mixing bowl.
3. Add the sliced strawberries to the bowl.
4. Toss in the halved orange slices.
5. Add the sliced olives and chopped nuts.
6. Drizzle the thick honey sauce over the salad.
7. Add the thick mustard sauce next, making sure to evenly distribute it.
8. Lastly, add the liquid oil, and gently toss the ingredients together until everything is well-coated.
9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
